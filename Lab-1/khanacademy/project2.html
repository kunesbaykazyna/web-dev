1.Banana smoothie

Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantities:
Banana 1
Milk 250ml
Honey 1

Steps:

  1. Cut the banana into pieces.
  2. Put the banana in a blender, add milk and honey.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into a glass and drink immediately.

3.Avocado Toast

Ingredients:

Ingredients Quantities:
bread 1
avocado 1
salt 1
lemon juice 10ml

Steps:

  1. Toast the bread in a toaster or on a pan..
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork and add salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  3. Spread the avocado on the toast.
  4. Eat immediately.
